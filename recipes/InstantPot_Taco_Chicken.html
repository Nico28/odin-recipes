InstantPot Taco Chicken



These fantastic shreded chicken tacos are a fun, healty, and fast week night dinner that can be done in around 30 minues!


Ingredients


  • Three boneless skinless chicken brests
  • 8oz for your favorite salsa
  • One cup of chicken broth
  • Teaspoon of cumin
  • Teaspoon of chili powder
  • Half teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Half teaspoonof onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. Lay your chicken brests side up in your InstantPot and season with salt, pepper, and all spices. a
  2. Pour in chicken broth and salsa.
  3. Cover lid, press the presure cook option on high.(This depends on your InstantPot intructions.)
  4. Close steam valuve and set timer for 14 minues.
  5. After InstantPot has naturaly realiesed shreed chicken and enjoy!
