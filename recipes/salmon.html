Salmon with Sour Cream Dill Sauce


This salmon with a sour cream dill sauce is a heathly, quick, and easy,
dinner that your family will love.


Ingredients


  • Two 8oz salmon fillets
  • Four tablespoons of sour cream
  • One tablespoon dejion mustard
  • Two teaspoons of dill divided
  • One lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • One tablespoon of high temp oil. Canola, walnut, or avacado

  1. In a bowl mix sourcream, mustard, teaspoon dill, salt and pepper to taste. Chill
  2. Add a little oil to salmon then season with rest of dill, salt and pepper to taste
  3. Turn in stove to medium high heat, place oil in non-stick pan to oil shimmers
  4. Place salmon skin side down and cook salmon 75% this way. Then flip and cook to 125ยบF/50ยบ internal temp. Rest five minutes.
  5. Plate salmon and sauce squeeze lemon to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
