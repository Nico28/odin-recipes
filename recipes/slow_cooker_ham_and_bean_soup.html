Slow Cooker Ham and Bean Soup


This hearthy ham and bead soup recipe is perfect for cold winter nights. Add a crusty baguette and a simple arugula salad.


Equipment

  • Slow Cooker or Crock Pot over 4 qts.

Ingredients

  • 1lbs. dry great northern or navy beans.
  • 3-4lbs. smoked ham shank.
  • 1 whole white or yellow onion.
  • 3-4 stalks of celery.
  • salt and pepper to taste.
  • Water to fill.

Directions

  1. Fine dice onion and celery
  2. In slow cooker or Crock Pot put in ham shank, beans, onions and celery, peper to taste. Cover with Water
  3. Cook on high for four hours. Cook on low for six to eight hours.
  4. Remove ham shank bone shreed meat and return to pot.
  5. Add salt to taste as ham shank is already salty.
  6. Enjoy!
